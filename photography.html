Photography is the perfect medium to reflect on, and to document, our relation to time and space. This collection of photostories is built upon the situations that made me stop, stand still and observe; situations to which I have kept on returning, each time discovering new nuances.

Film Photography section presents images that were created as “film stills”. Still photographic images from film production are used for promotion or for documentation that assists the production team in providing coherence to the scenography and costume details.
