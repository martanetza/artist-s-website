One man said: “animals are human's best friends”. However this friendship is not based on loyalty or trust. Animals are the creatures that live their life without: politics, money, rush for career and widely understood success. They are like children that remind us of a happy joyful life. Life that can be in a present time without reconsidering too much of the past and dreaming about the future.

Having a chance to portrait the one that you love most, who would you choose, your wife, parents or your dog?

I am not only interested in the traditional focus on the form of beauty. Instead, I am concentrating on capturing the personality and nature of portrayed persons and animals, which emerges in their everyday, seemingly meaningless gestures and behaviors. Therefore, in the part that is preceding the painting process I take photos by myself. Thus the portraits are results of observation and reflection, they are becoming a place where you can mirror yourself and reflect on beloved in.
