vejlands atelier

Vejlands atelier is a green house, built with drawing, painting and socializing in mind. I would be very glad to share it with other creative souls.

EVENTS

Join my Facebook group, where I will be creating events, such as vernissages, creative workshops and alike. By the way, the opening event of Vejlands atelier is coming soon!

CLASSES

I am offering drawing and painting classes at various levels, both for groups and individually. I have in the past taught drawing and painting techniques in Germany, which I highly enjoyed. Contact me and we will discuss in more detail.

RENT

Need a place to concentrate on your creative endeavours? My atelier will be available for rent during most of the weekends and it is donation based. Contact me and we will discuss in more detail.
